Platform Overview: CoSolve is a platform designed to connect individuals facing challenges with those who can provide solutions, fostering collaboration and mutual support.

Key Features: Combines elements of Instagram, LinkedIn, Quora, and Freelancer. Enables users to post requests or solutions in areas like transportation, education, employment, and more. Facilitates knowledge sharing, professional networking, and freelancing opportunities.

Examples of Applications:
 Vehicle sharing for students to earn extra income. 
Connecting skilled students with professionals for project-based work.
Improving rural education by linking unemployed individuals with teaching roles.
